Help me describe a UI that looks like a stack of papers on a desk.
assistant

To capture that aesthetic, focus on three key elements:

  1. Shadows & Depth — Use diffused, large-spread shadows (box-shadow) to separate white "paper" containers from the warm grey background. Avoid harsh borders.
  2. Geometry — Sharp corners or extremely subtle border-radius (1–2px) reinforce the physical paper feel compared to the rounded digital standard.
  3. Typography — Keep text small, crisp, and high-contrast (dark grey on white). Use lowercase headers to create an understated, editorial vibe.

The ::before and ::after pseudo-elements are your best tool. By positioning them behind the main card at slight rotation offsets, you create the visual impression of multiple sheets without extra DOM elements.
